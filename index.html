OUR WORKSHOPS ARE BUILD AROUND THREE MULTI-PREFIXES.
MULTICULTURAL, MULTILINGUAL AND MULTIDISCIPLINARY




ETFI is the coordinator of the Interdisciplinary Dialogue, a project supported
by the European Comission and bringing together five Art universities
from Europe and Oceania, and 10 associated partners from across Europe.
Within the framework of the THEATER in PALM project, supported
by the Creative Europe program, ETFI represents Belgium in a pan-European
platform supporting emerging artists.

As a partner in the Erasmus+ Transit On Stage,
ETFI supports the Life Long Learning program for adults.
Thus, under the umbrella of these three projects,
ETFI reaches 30,000 students and more than
200 educators and theater, music and film professionals.
